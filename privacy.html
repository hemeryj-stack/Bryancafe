Privacy Policy

Bryan’s Café respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information collected through the enquiry form on this website.

When you submit an enquiry, we may collect your name, email address, message subject and message content. This information is used only to respond to your enquiry and provide customer support.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

Bryan’s Café does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared where required by law or where necessary to respond to your request.

You may contact Bryan’s Café if you would like to request access to or correction of your personal information.
